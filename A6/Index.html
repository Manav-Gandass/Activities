What Youâ€™ll Need

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

How to Make Them

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ones and stir until the batter is smooth.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat, then scoop batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on top, flip, and continue cooking until golden brown.
